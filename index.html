Excedption Games — Privacy Policy

1. Games Covered

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") applies to all mobile games published by Excedption on Google Play and the Apple App Store, including:

When a new game is released under the same publisher, this Policy automatically applies to it unless we publish a separate policy and link to it from the game's store listing.

2. Summary (Plain English)

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or location.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • Your game progress (scores, settings) is saved only on your device.
  • We show ads through Unity Ads (Unity Technologies), which uses an anonymous advertising ID to show you relevant ads.
  • You can disable personalized ads at any time in your device settings.
  • Removing ads through in-app purchase stops ad-serving but does not change this policy.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Locally on Your Device (Not Sent to Us)

  • Game progress, best scores, streaks, unlocked stages
  • In-app settings (sound on/off, music on/off, vibration on/off)
  • Whether you purchased "Remove Ads"
  • Whether you have seen the tutorial

This data is stored in your device's local storage and is never transmitted to our servers. If you uninstall the game, this data is deleted.

3.2 Collected by Third-Party Services

The following data is collected by Unity Ads when ads are shown:

  • Advertising identifier — IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android (you can reset this at any time)
  • Approximate location — derived from IP address (country / region level, not precise)
  • Device information — device model, operating system version, screen size, language setting
  • Ad interaction data — which ads were shown, clicks, dwell time
  • Crash and diagnostic logs — for SDK stability

We do not have direct access to this raw data. It is collected and processed by Unity Technologies under its own privacy policy: unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy.

3.3 Processed by the App Stores (Not by Us)

When you install or purchase our apps, the Apple App Store and Google Play process billing and device-identity data according to their own policies. We receive only aggregated, non-personally-identifiable sales reports.

4. How We Use Data

  • To display ads and measure their effectiveness (via Unity Ads)
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability
  • To verify your "Remove Ads" purchase and disable ad-serving accordingly

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use data for any purpose other than those listed above.

5. Third-Party Services

Our games use the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Unity Ads (advertising, by Unity Technologies) — unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy
  • Google Play Billing / Apple StoreKit (in-app purchase) — processed by the respective store
  • Apple SKAdNetwork (iOS 14.5+ privacy-preserving ad attribution) — processed by Apple

We may add or change third-party services in the future. When we do, this Policy will be updated.

5A. Ad Formats Used

Our games use the following ad formats delivered by Unity Ads:

  • Banner ads — Small ads shown at the bottom of the screen during gameplay. Always visible unless removed by in-app purchase.
  • Interstitial ads — Full-screen ads shown between game sessions, typically after a game-over event. Frequency is capped to at most one every 60 seconds.
  • Rewarded ads — Optional video ads the player may choose to watch in exchange for an in-game reward (e.g., extra life). Always initiated by the user; never forced.

Rewarded ads are considered user-initiated. Even if you decline Ad Tracking, you may still choose to watch rewarded ads; doing so will not cause our games to track you across other apps or websites.

Ad content is filtered by Unity Ads according to the age rating of our games. We do not allow advertising categories that are unsuitable for a general audience (for example, gambling, alcohol, or explicit content).

6. Your Choices and Rights

6.1 Opt Out of Personalized Ads

iOS (14.5 and later): The first time you open the app you will see an "Allow this app to track your activity?" prompt. If you choose "Ask App Not to Track", Unity Ads will show non-personalized ads only. You can also change this at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Android: You can opt out of ad personalization at the OS level in Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization. You can also reset your Advertising ID from the same screen. Unity Ads honors this signal and will switch to non-personalized (contextual) ads.

6.2 Delete Your Data

Because we do not store any personally identifiable data on our servers, the only data related to you exists on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. To reset ad-serving history, reset your device-level Advertising Identifier (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking; Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

6.3 Request Information

You can contact us at any time at excedption@gmail.com to ask what data (if any) is associated with your advertising ID. We will respond within 30 days.

7. EU / UK Users (GDPR)

If you access our games from the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we will ask for your consent before personalized ads are shown, in accordance with Unity Ads' GDPR compliance framework. Your choices:

  • Consent — Unity Ads shows personalized ads based on your interests.
  • Do not consent — Unity Ads shows only non-personalized (contextual) ads.

You can revisit this choice at any time from the game's in-app Settings screen (where available) or by reinstalling the game. Under GDPR you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and objection. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email above.

Legal basis for processing: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) for personalized ads, and our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) for non-personalized ads, crash diagnostics, and fraud prevention.

8. California Users (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what categories of personal information are collected about you
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • Delete personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

We do not sell personal information in the traditional sense. However, Unity Ads' use of your advertising identifier for personalized advertising may be considered "sharing" under CPRA. You can opt out via the device-level controls described in Section 6.1, or by contacting us.
